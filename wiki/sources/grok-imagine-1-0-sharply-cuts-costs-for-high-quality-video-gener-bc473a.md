xAI launched a video generator that topped an independent quality ranking at a fraction of competitors’ prices.
What’s new: [Grok Imagine 1.0](https://x.ai/news/grok-imagine-api?utm_campaign=The%20Batch&utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-9o2Recq645Sh94dkQERF-s-TPwoF7fmy7Bw20yk8Bg7em33UUSAF9nnQ3hCwDYUj56R_zz) takes text with images and/or video, and produces video clips that can include dialogue, sound effects, and music.
- Input/output: Text, image (optional), video (optional) in, video with audio out (up to 10 seconds at 1,280x720 pixels via chat interfaces, up to 15 seconds at 1,280x720 pixels or 854x480 pixels via API)
- Performance: Topped Artificial Analysis Video Arena in both text-to-video and image-to-video at launch
- Capabilities: Video alteration via text instructions, camera motion (pan, tilt, zoom); add, remove, and swap objects within scenes; style transfer; multiple aspect ratios
- Availability/price: Web interface via grok.com, x.com, and Grok mobile app (free for X Basic and Premium users; Premium users can generate longer videos),
[API](https://docs.x.ai/developers/model-capabilities/video/generation?utm_campaign=The%20Batch&utm_source=hs_email&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-9o2Recq645Sh94dkQERF-s-TPwoF7fmy7Bw20yk8Bg7em33UUSAF9nnQ3hCwDYUj56R_zz)$4.20 per minute of output - Undisclosed: xAI disclosed no information about Grok Imagine 1.0’s underlying technology and how it was built.
