HEX is a Vision-Language-Action framework developed by Beijing Innovation Center of Humanoid Robotics and collaborators, designed for full-sized bipedal humanoid robots to perform coordinated whole-body manipulation. It introduces a humanoid-aligned state representation and a Mixture-of-Experts Unified Proprioceptive Predictor to enable cross-embodiment learning and achieved an average 79.8% success rate across real-robot tasks, outperforming existing baselines in generalization and long-horizon scenarios.
