Researchers from Alibaba Group and Huazhong University of Science and Technology introduce the Hierarchical Decoupled Policy Optimization (HDPO) framework to enable agentic multimodal models to strategically arbitrate between internal knowledge and external tools. Their model, Metis, significantly reduces unnecessary tool invocations (e.g., from 98% to 2% on V*Bench) while simultaneously achieving state-of-the-art accuracy on various multimodal reasoning benchmarks.
