VINTIX II scales the Decision Pre-Trained Transformer framework for in-context reinforcement learning to diverse, continuous-control environments by integrating a flow-based policy head. The system demonstrates enhanced generalization and adaptive capabilities on both seen and unseen tasks, outperforming prior large action models.
