Researchers from Zhejiang University, Apple, and Tencent developed KnowU-Bench, a new benchmark for evaluating mobile agents' personalized and proactive capabilities in a live Android emulation environment. This platform, featuring an LLM-driven user simulator, revealed substantial performance gaps in state-of-the-art models when tasks required user preference inference or calibrated proactive decisions.
