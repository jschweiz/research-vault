Researchers at Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory and Shanghai Jiao Tong University found that supervised fine-tuning (SFT) can foster cross-domain generalization in large language models for reasoning tasks, provided there is sufficient optimization, high-quality long chain-of-thought (CoT) data, and capable base models. This reasoning generalization, however, was observed to correlate with a decrease in model safety.
