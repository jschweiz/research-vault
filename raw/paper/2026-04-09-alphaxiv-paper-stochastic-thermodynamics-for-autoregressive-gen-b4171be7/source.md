Takahiro Sagawa developed a stochastic thermodynamic framework for non-Markovian autoregressive generative models, including Transformers, enabling efficient computation of entropy production from sampled trajectories. Applying the framework to GPT-2, it revealed that coarse-grained entropy production can differentiate between causal and non-causal text structures.
