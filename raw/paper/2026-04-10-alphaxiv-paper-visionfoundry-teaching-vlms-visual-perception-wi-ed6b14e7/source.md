VisionFoundry presents an automated pipeline for generating task-targeted synthetic visual question answering data, using only a keyword, to enhance visual perception in vision-language models. The approach consistently improves VLM performance on diagnostic benchmarks such as MMVP and CV-Bench by up to +10%, while generally maintaining broader capabilities.
