Robotic machine learning company Generalist's GEN-1 physical AI system reportedly crosses into production-level success rates on a broad range of physical skills. The model is also able to respond to disruptions by improvising new moves. The company collected over half a million hours and petabytes of physical interaction data to help train the model.
